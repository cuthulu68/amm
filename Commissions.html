IMPORTANT: Everything I make is completely custom and some options, such as bone ash glazes require more time for testing. Please look on the process page for details. Because of this it takes two to three months from time a deposit is placed until it ships. Every piece I am commissioned for I take seriously and treat with respect. This takes time.

How do commissions work? All work requires a deposit. Deposits are 50% of the total price of the work sans shipping. After You've made a deposit I like to sit down on a half hour call or video call and discuss who you want to memorialize and what they were like. What makes you smile when you think of them. Was there something that was special between the two of you. For example, I made a coffee mug with cremains in the clay and bone ash glaze. A mug was selected becuase they would get up every morning, let their dog out and then have a cup of coffee. The mug is a way that they continue to spend the morning together. Another person loved to make a big bowl of ramen, sit in the dark with her dog and watch a horror movie. So in there case I made a ramen bowl so she can still have ramen with her dog.b>
